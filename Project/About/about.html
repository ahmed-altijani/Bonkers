About Friskay Company

Welcome to Friskay, where our passion for feline well-being drives everything we do. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to creating high-quality, nutritious, and delicious cat food through our Bonkers brand. From flavor innovation to sustainable practices, Friskay is committed to providing cats with the best meals while keeping our planet in mind.

Our Mission

At Friskay, our mission is simple: to enhance the health and happiness of every cat. We believe that a balanced diet is key to a long and joyful life for our feline companions, and that’s why we put so much care into crafting each product.

Our Story

Friskay was founded by a group of pet lovers who noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, responsibly sourced cat food. Driven by a desire to offer only the best, we created the Bonkers line of cat food, featuring unique flavor combinations and nutrient-packed formulas. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted brand for cat parents everywhere.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Flavor Innovation: We constantly push the boundaries with flavor combinations like "Tuna & Catnip Delight" and "Chicken & Pumpkin Medley" that cats love.
  • Health-Focused Recipes: Our Nutri-Paws Formula, filled with probiotics and omega fatty acids, ensures digestive health and a shiny coat.
  • Eco-Friendly Commitment: We source all of our ingredients responsibly, and our packaging is designed to minimize environmental impact.
  • Community-Centered: A portion of every purchase is donated to local animal shelters and rescue organizations.

Join the Bonkers Adventure

We’re not just a cat food brand; we’re a community. Through the Bonkers Adventure Club, our customers can share their cats’ playful moments, participate in fun contests, and access exclusive content. Together, we celebrate the bond between cats and their owners.

Thank you for choosing Friskay and trusting us to care for your cat’s nutrition. We look forward to continuing this adventure with you and your feline friend!
