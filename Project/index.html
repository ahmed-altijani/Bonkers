Why Choose Cat Food?

Flavor Fusion

Bonkers offers unique flavor combinations, such as "Tuna & Catnip Delight" and "Chicken & Pumpkin Medley," designed to excite your cat's palate and provide diverse nutritional benefits.

Nutri-Paws Formula

Each Bonkers recipe is crafted with a special blend of probiotics and omega fatty acids, promoting digestive health and a shiny coat, ensuring your cat feels as good as it tastes!

Eco-Friendly Ingredients

Friskay sources all ingredients sustainably, ensuring that Bonkers not only supports your cat's health but also the health of the planet. All fish is caught through responsible fishing practices.

No Artificial Additives

Bonkers is completely free from artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives. Each recipe is made from whole, recognizable ingredients that you can feel good about feeding your cat.

Interactive Feeding

Bonkers offers an interactive feeding experience through its packaging. Each box includes fun cat games and tips on how to keep your feline engaged during meal times.

Special Diet Options

Bonkers has a range of specialized diets, including grain-free, high-protein, and sensitive stomach formulas, ensuring that every cat, regardless of dietary needs, can enjoy Bonkers.

Cats’ Choice Award

Bonkers has won several fictional awards, including the “Best Cat Food of the Year” and the “Purr-fect Taste Award,” voted by cats themselves in whimsical polls held by Friskay.

Community Focus

For every purchase of Bonkers, Friskay donates a portion of the proceeds to local animal shelters and rescue organizations, helping to provide food and care for cats in need.

Bonkers Adventure Club

Friskay hosts an online community called the Bonkers Adventure Club, where cat owners can share their pets' playful moments, join contests, and access exclusive discounts on products.

Whimsical Mascot

The Bonkers brand features a quirky mascot named Captain Whiskers, a playful cartoon cat who leads the charge on all Bonkers promotions, including special treats and seasonal flavors.
