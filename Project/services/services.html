Our Services

At Friskay, we’re dedicated to enhancing your cat’s health and happiness through our flagship product line, Bonkers Cat Food. Explore our variety of services and find the perfect solution for your feline friend.

Bonkers Cat Food Varieties

Friskay provides a range of specially crafted cat food formulas under the Bonkers brand:

  • Tuna & Catnip Delight: A blend of fresh tuna and catnip, perfect for playful cats.
  • Chicken & Pumpkin Medley: Protein-rich chicken and pumpkin for healthy digestion.
  • Grain-Free Options: High-protein formulas for cats with grain sensitivities.
  • Sensitive Stomach Formula: Gentle ingredients that aid in digestion for sensitive cats.
  • Senior Cat Formula: A specialized blend for older cats with joint-supporting nutrients.

Bonkers Subscription Service
